The Project

Why This Revamp

ShareChat has the scale. It needs the experience to match.

The Opportunity

ShareChat serves 325 million monthly active users across 15+ Indic languages — yet its user experience has not kept pace with the expectations of a mobile-first, video-first generation.

Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts have set a new bar. Bharat's creators and consumers deserve a platform that matches their ambition — one that is beautiful, fast, and deeply culturally relevant.

This proposal reimagines ShareChat's core experience: a dark, immersive video feed; AI-powered discovery; a creator monetization layer; and a design system that feels premium across every interaction.

📱

Vertical-first video feed

Scroll-snap full-screen reels with pre-loaded next video — zero wait, zero friction.

🎨

Premium dark UI

Glassmorphism, micro-animations, and OLED-friendly dark mode — a true design-system upgrade.

🧠

AI-powered discovery

Personalised topic chips, trending sounds, and a masonry grid that learns what you love.

💰

Creator economy

In-app UPI tipping, live gifts, a monetisation dashboard, and a brand marketplace.

🌍

Seamless multilingual UX

Auto-detect language from content, transliteration, and voice-first search for 1B+ Bharat users.
