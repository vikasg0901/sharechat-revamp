What Changes

Every Detail, Reimagined

A complete UX overhaul across navigation, content consumption, creation, and monetization.

📱
Vertical-First Video Feed
TikTok-style scroll-snap video feed with full-screen immersive playback. Swipe up to discover, tap to engage — no clunky grid-to-player transitions. Pre-loaded next videos for zero-wait scrolling.
🎨
Dark Mode Native Design
A premium dark-first UI with glassmorphism, subtle gradients, and OLED-friendly blacks. Reduces eye strain, saves battery, and screams premium — exactly what Gen Z expects from a modern social app.
⚡
Stories + Fleets Layer
Ephemeral content at the top of every feed. Stories drive daily opens and creator-to-follower intimacy. Support for polls, Q&A, countdowns, and music stickers — all localized in 15+ languages.
🧠
AI-Powered Discovery
A "Discover" tab powered by collaborative filtering + content understanding. Personalized topic chips, trending sounds, creator spotlights, and a masonry grid that adapts to what you love.
💰
Creator Economy Suite
In-app tipping with UPI, virtual gifts during live streams, a creator dashboard with analytics, brand partnership marketplace, and revenue sharing — making ShareChat the place creators choose first.
🌍
Seamless Multilingual UX
Auto-detect language from content, not settings. Mixed-language feeds that feel natural. Transliteration support, voice-first search for non-keyboard users, and culturally adaptive UI components.
